Game Introduction

Flip the cards to find matching images. Each image offers a hint about different perspectives in life. Pay attention, as there’s a hidden message behind the pairs. Complete the game to uncover the mystery theme!

Game Rules

  • Click on a card to reveal a hidden image.
  • Match pairs of identical images to score points.
  • Correct matches increase your score; mistakes reduce it slightly.
  • Finish quickly to maximize your score and reveal the theme!
